🏠 Before the storm hits

What is a Category 1 shelter?

Category 1 shelters are the strongest public buildings in Barbados, certified by DEM to withstand hurricane-force winds. They are activated only when DEM says so. If you live in a solid concrete house away from the coast, you may not need to go. If you live in a wooden house, near the coast, or in a low-lying area, a Category 1 shelter is your safest option.

2026 Atlantic hurricane season

Hurricane season runs from 1 June to 30 November 2026. Names are set by the World Meteorological Organisation and confirmed by the Barbados Meteorological Services.

Hurricane Melissa (2025) — a Category 5 storm that affected more than 626,000 people in Jamaica, causing an estimated US$8–15 billion in damage. The name Melissa has been retired. It is replaced in the 2031 cycle. The 2026 list is in use for the first time.
